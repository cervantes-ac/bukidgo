# Bukidnon Theme - Visual Design Reference\n\n## Color Palette Reference\n\n### Primary Colors (Mountain & Forest)\n```\n🟩 Dark Green (#0A3D2F)      - Primary background, deep forest\n🟩 Forest Green (#1E4D2B)    - Mountain accent, hover states\n🟩 Mountain Green (#2D6A4F)  - Secondary accent\n🟩 Light Green (#4A9D6F)     - Interactive elements, success\n```\n\n### Accent Colors (Tribal & Cultural)\n```\n🟨 Gold (#F5C400)            - Primary accent, highlights\n🟨 Gold Light (#FDD835)      - Hover states, lighter accents\n```\n\n### Earth Tones (Natural)\n```\n🟫 Earth Brown (#8B4513)      - Text, borders, secondary buttons\n🟫 Brown Accent (#C4622D)     - Warm accents, badges\n🟫 Dark Brown (#5D3A1A)       - Deep text, strong emphasis\n```\n\n### Neutral Palette (Background & Text)\n```\n⬜ Cream (#F5F5F0)            - Main background\n⬜ Light Cream (#F5F0E8)      - Secondary background\n⬜ Beige (#E8DCC8)            - Borders, dividers\n⬜ Dark Text (#1A1208)        - Primary text\n⬜ Medium Text (#2D2D2D)      - Secondary text\n⬜ Light Text (rgba...)       - Tertiary text, disabled\n```\n\n## Typography System\n\n### Font Stack\n```\nSerif:      Playfair Display, elements\n5. **Accessibility**: Inclusive design for all users\n6. **Responsive**: Works seamlessly on all devices\n7. **Performance**: Optimized animations and transitions\n8. **Consistency**:
